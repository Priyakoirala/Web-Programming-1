Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What payment methods are accepted?

At Elegant Ensembles, we accept a variety of payment options, including credit/debit cards, online banking, and mobile wallets.

How can I track my order?

After your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery at Elegant Ensembles.

What is your return policy?

At Elegant Ensembles, you may return products within 30 days from receipt, ensuring they are returned in their original condition.

Do you offer international shipping?

Unfortunately, we currently only ship within Nepal and do not offer international shipping options.

How can I contact customer support?

For assistance, please visit our website's Contact Us page to connect with our dedicated customer support team.

What should I do if I receive a damaged product?

In the event of receiving a damaged product, kindly reach out to us promptly via our Contact Us page. Please include your order details along with photos of the damage for our immediate assistance.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

You may modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Please contact our customer support team promptly for assistance.

Do you have a physical store?

Certainly! Our ElegantEnsembles Clothing Store is situated in Kadaghari, Kathmandu. You are welcome to visit us any time between 10 AM and 8 PM during our regular hours.
